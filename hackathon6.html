SharXathon: Future Forward

Join us for a 48-hour sprint of innovation and creativity, where you'll build the future of technology. Compete for prizes, network with industry leaders, and showcase your skills.

Overview Rules Judges Winners

SharXathon Rules

1. Eligibility

Participants must be 18 years or older. All skill levels are welcome. You can participate individually or in teams of up to 4 members.

2. Project Submission

All projects must be submitted by the specified deadline. Submissions should include a link to a public code repository (e.g., GitHub) and a short video demo (max 3 minutes).

3. Code of Conduct

All participants must adhere to our code of conduct. We are committed to providing a safe and inclusive environment for everyone. Harassment or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.

4. Originality

All work must be original and created during the SharXathon. Use of third-party libraries and frameworks is permitted, but the core idea and implementation must be your own.

5. Intellectual Property

Teams retain full ownership of their projects and intellectual property. By submitting, you grant us permission to showcase your project for promotional purposes.

Prizes

1st Place: $5,000 + Mentorship from Industry Experts

2nd Place: $3,000 + Access to Premium Resources

3rd Place: $2,000 + Networking Opportunities

Judging Criteria

Projects will be evaluated based on:

  • Innovation (30%)
  • Technical Implementation (30%)
  • Impact and Relevance (20%)
  • Presentation and Demo (20%)
